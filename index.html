Instructions

A random number will appear: the goal is to match this number without exceeding it.

There are four crystals (as you can see, duh). When you click a crystal, a specific amount of points will be added to your score.

If you match the number, you win! If you pass it, you lose because our alien overlords have declared it so.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Don't mess this up, okay? It's not that hard.
